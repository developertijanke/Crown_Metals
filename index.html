Explore Our Trading Services

Gold Trading

We facilitate the sale of high-quality gold for remelting to investment-grade standards (999.95 and higher). Our transactions ensure secure and ethical sourcing, complying with OECD guidelines, and involve escrow accounts for client safety.

Learn More
Diamonds Trading

We offer raw and certified diamonds with competitive discounts from Rapaport prices. Transactions are conducted in secure locations such as Antwerp, Dubai, Tel Aviv, and Nairobi, ensuring authenticity through gemmological verification.

Learn More
Copper Cathodes Trading

With the rising demand for copper, we supply high-quality copper cathodes at competitive prices under CIF or FOB terms. All transactions are secured through international bank guarantees, ensuring a transparent process.

Learn More
Fuel Trading

We source and supply a wide range of fuels, including diesel (EN-590), petrol, LPG, LNG, and jet fuel, with excellent discounts from Platts prices. Our network ensures reliable and efficient delivery across multiple locations.

Learn More
Sugar Trading

We provide high-quality ICUMSA 45 sugar at competitive CIF or FOB terms. Buyers can benefit from secure payment options and thorough quality checks conducted by SGS before shipment.

Private Aircraft & Helicopters

A Gold Supply Chain Network With A Difference

We are passionate about responsible sourcing of gold and precious metals in Africa. It is this passion that made us the market leaders in our industry. We have a dominant position in the procurement and supply of raw gold, copper cathodes, and rough diamonds, which are supplied to the international market at competitive prices.
